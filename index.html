About Me

Dave Meneses was born in Chicago on September 10, 1990. He grew up in the city and the suburbs and eventually went to college in Deerfield, IL where he graduated with a degrees in Humanities and Secondary Education. After school, he had brief stints as a middle school teacher and a professional fundraiser before he left the world of education and started working in the service industry.

Coming from a unique background, he was able to use his education experience to become a trainer in the beverage world and helped coached competitors at the highest level of coffee competition. Bouncing back and forth between Chicago and LA, he worked with some of the most respected coffee brands and grew his role to be the Midwest Area Manager for Stumptown Coffee.

His hobbies include many physically taxing sports including skateboarding, rugby, and jiu jitsu. Unfortunately, it was that love for the extreme that caused him to have a serious injury, tearing both a tendon and a ligament in his ankle. Luckily, during his extended time off he was able to rediscover his love for an old hobby of his, coding. Because his recovery time was estimated to take over a year he decided it was time for a change and signed up for Northwestern’s Coding Bootcamp where he is learning to turn his old hobby into a profession.
